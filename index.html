    🌸 Meet Nanna — Iola Herschell, RN

    Iola Herschell is a licensed Registered Nurse who devoted over 25 years of her life to clinical nursing practice, caring for patients across a wide range of settings with deep compassion and medical expertise. After retiring from her nursing career, Iola's two greatest passions — helping others and her love of gardening — naturally converged on a path she hadn't expected: the ancient and deeply personal world of home remedies and herbal medicine.

    What began as a personal quest to replace her own family's prescription medications with documented, carefully researched herbal alternatives became a mission. Through dedicated herbalist coursework, hands-on experience cultivating medicinal plants, and applying her 25+ years of clinical knowledge, Iola built a body of work that is both medically grounded and rooted in the warm, commonsense wisdom that only comes from a lifetime of caring for people.

    Nanna's Herbal Apothecary was born from that mission — to share that knowledge with families, wellness seekers, and anyone who wants safer, more natural options without sacrificing credibility or safety. Every recommendation on this site is tied to peer-reviewed medical research and reviewed through Iola's clinical RN lens.
